HOTEL IN SRI LANKA

Our Homes Of Sri Lankan Hospitality Spread across the length and breadth of our tropical island, our homes of Sri Lankan hospitality invite you to indulge in our signature brand of luxury, brought to life with the strength of our well-rooted family values. No matter where you may find yourself in Sri Lanka, our doors will always be open. Nurtured over generations, this innate sense of warmth and care welcomes you to a variety of enchanting landscapes that inspire our hotels in Sri Lanka. we have since extended our family to all sides of the island in order to bring you the finest of Sri Lankan hospitality.

The land of our founder’s home in the capital of Colombo, we bring you urban novelty built on family heritage. you have the choice of many sunny settings, including a secluded cove and a magnificent hillock. as we enter uncharted territory that extends to the pristine shores of our untouched east coast, while the top of our teardrop-shaped isle features the great northern peninsula rich in colourful history that is rivaled only by the ancient kingdoms of our cultural triangle and the colonial legacies of our central highlands. Through the hundreds and thousands of stories that fuel the magic of our land, our many homes welcome you to share in the legendary comforts of our Sri Lankan hospitality.
